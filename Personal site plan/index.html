Idaho Hunting Outdoors Site Plan

Connor Wood

WDD 131

Purpose

To provide a comprehensive resource for Idaho hunting enthusiasts, offering information on hunting seasons, regulations, locations, tips, and community forums. To foster a responsible and ethical hunting environment and promote the appreciation of Idaho's natural resources.

Audience

Idaho residents and non-resident hunters interested in hunting in Idaho. This includes both novice and experienced hunters, outdoor enthusiasts, and individuals seeking information on hunting regulations and opportunities. They are likely tech-savvy, using mobile devices and computers for information gathering.

Dynamic Elements

  • Interactive map displaying hunting zones and public land boundaries.
  • Real-time updates on hunting season dates and closures.
  • Community forum for hunters to share experiences and tips.
  • Dynamic display of weather forecasts for hunting locations.
  • A gallery of user submitted photos of hunts.
  • A tool to calculate required tags and permits.

Content

Home Page

Introduction to Idaho hunting, featured hunting locations, recent hunting reports, upcoming hunting seasons, and links to forum and resources.

Hunting Regulations

Detailed information on Idaho hunting regulations, license requirements, and safety guidelines.

Hunting Locations

Interactive map and detailed descriptions of popular hunting locations in Idaho, including access points and terrain information.

Community Forum

A forum for hunters to discuss hunting strategies, share stories, and ask questions.

Resources

Links to Idaho Fish and Game, weather forecasts, gear recommendations, and other useful information.
